Putin ‘seriously considering’ visit to Malaysia, says Anwar

However, the prime minister did not provide a timeline for the proposed visit.

KUALA LUMPUR: Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim said Russian president Vladimir Putin is “seriously considering” visiting Malaysia.
“President Putin met me in Beijing and said he is seriously considering coming,” Anwar said at AmBank’s 50th anniversary celebration.
However, Anwar did not provide a timeline for Putin’s proposed visit.
